Echoes of Heartache: A Collection of Tear-Stained Love Verses

1.1 - Stellar Serenade by Noah De Belen

Through wooded lanes and wealth's abode we've been,
In jest, claimed they were beyond our amuse,
Socially matched, with backgrounds grim and thin,
Fathers that fled, and mothers left to booze.
A timeless tale, wherein love never lasts,
From afar, I yearn for what was, a tryst,
Yet now, in the present, I look upon the past,
A once-known soul, lost in life's lone abyss.
I mused, wandering, would bring love anew,
Said, "Distance breeds fondness," yet love departed,
The sole mistake, not fleeing love's pursue,
Now see the havoc that left us brokenhearted.
O'er seas and stars, we rode, love's own chart,
From Saturn's rings to Mars, love, worlds apart.

1.2 - Astronomy by Conan Gray

We drive through the woods, rich neighborhoods to watch
We joked as we looked that they were too good for us
'Cause socially speaking, we were the same
With runaway fathers and mothers who drank
A tale old as time, young love don't last for life
From far away, I wish I'd stayed with you
But here, face to face, a stranger that I once knew
I thought if I wandered, I'd fall back in love
You said distance brings fondness, but guess not with us
The only mistake that we didn't make was run
(Now look what we've done)
We've traveled the seas, we've ridden the stars
We've seen everything from Saturn to Mars
As much as it seems like you own my heart
It's astronomy, we're two worlds apart

2.1 - Betrayal, Without Remorse by Noah De Belen

Brown eyes, guilt-laden, love whispered white lies,
I played the fool, aware, my heart askew,
To her, your words, perhaps actions untrue,
Silent, I stayed, my love veiled in disguise.
Betrayed, your actions like venomous spies,
Unrepentant gaze, remorse in review,
The pain you caused, my heart's nowhere anew,
Sorry eludes, in your cold callous lies.
Together, in your arms, I found my best,
Embraced you at your worst, love's sweetest taste,
Yet it mattered little, love put to test.
Two weeks passed, a new passion you embraced,
With no cheating's stain, but traitor's harsh jest,
In the ruins of trust, my heart misplaced

2.2 - Traitor by Olivia Rodrigo

Brown guilty eyes and little white lies, yeah
I played dumb, but I always knew
That you talked to her, maybe did even worse
I kept quiet so I could keep you
You betrayed me
And I know that you'll never feel sorry
For the way I hurt, yeah
You talked to her when we were together
Loved you at your worst, but that didn't matter
It took you two weeks to go off and date her
Guess you didn't cheat, but you're still a traitor

3.1 - Endless Wanderlust by Nino Malang

I sought for the troth of all my precious
All of my life, holding for a boundless fate
Enkindling twilight, the stars so callous
Journey to unravel, memories to create
With such small hope, our wills scream to prevail
Bearing the pain of man's consequences
Through God's true providence, we weep in frail
Still in the gist of us, we find solace in distress
A journey for truth, path to uncover
Amidst the gales of sorrow, we proclaim
Fighting for loves sake, light to recover
For eternal flames burn the passion we claim
In all of eternity, I will not forget
That say of hope and cheers for chalice I shall beget

3.2 - Forever Lost by Myth & Roid

I sighed that I lost something precious
I know that there may never be
An end to the price that we pay for our dreams
It can never be repaid to carry on is to carry a burden
In the twilight, we're praying for the lost
Fight all our grief, fight all our pain
To the fallen, we're hurting for the lost
Still we try to find solace, hand in hand together
In the twilight, we're going down this road
Full of your hope, full of your faith
To the fallen, we're going down this road
My journey has to go on with you
We will remember
That there's hope

4.1 - What could've been by Nino Malang

Yesterday all troubles, so far away
Suddenly, all thoughts of love flow my game
Day by day, relighting yesterday's blame
Thy love for thee was such a game to play
A place to shed tears and pray in dismay
Oh unfulfilling past, I trust aflame
Reminiscing treasures of the past, an undying flame
Wondering of the secrets that led me astray
Words, I have longed to say were such mistakes
For old day's amendment now deemed in pain
Thy love from the past, nothing but heartache
Nows a place to cover, but soon forsake
Relieving the demands of yesterday's vain
For yesterday's dream, I sought, for my sake

4.2 - Yesterday by The beatles

Yesterday all my troubles seemed so far away.
Now it looks as though they're here to stay.
Oh, I believe in yesterday.
Yesterday love was such an easy game to play.
Now I need a place to hide away.
Oh, I believe in yesterday.
Why she had to go?
I don't know, she wouldn't say.
I said something wrong.
Now I long for yesterday.
Yesterday love was such an easy game to play.
Now I need a place to hide away.
Oh, I believe in yesterday.
Now I long for yesterday.

5.1 - How Love Unravels by Ellizson Saez

With no cars on curb, wouldst I still be loved?
Lacking deep pockets, wouldst thou be loving?
Speaketh truth, I prefer trust, tis above
Keep it sincere', don't hast thee, hath nothing
Bestow upon me a contest for wealth
There is nobody, none to outrun me
So grant me a challenge for my gold-held
Feeling energized, feeling full of glee
Amidst a life adorned with beauty's grace
Doth love me completely with all thy heart
Beseech thee, let not passion's flame efface
Seeking only you, love me, never part
To be with thee, I plead, love me with ease
In thy arms I need, prithee, love me, please

6.1 - Love's Fault Line by Ellizson Saez

For thou cause my world to tremble and quake
Thou cause seismic waves of love and desire
Around we ride, your love shakes me afire
And 'tis tearing my heart to bitter break
Thy love, the cause of earth's deep, quaking shake
Oh, thou make my world shake with love's fine choir
Riding around, your love shakes and inspires
My heart falters, casting shadows and aches
No confrontation seeks my craving heart
Communication, not thy heart's ideal
Seeking confirmation of thy love's part
No more distractions, let our trust be real
'Tis my fault, I beg you not to depart
When all caves in, in thou I find appeal
