Extra-curricular Activities

Badminton Club

Welcome to our badminton club, where skill and passion collide as shuttlecocks are expertly
thrown into the air! Our club offers a lively and welcoming environment for badminton fans of
all skill levels, whether you're an experienced
player or a novice seeking to take up a racket for the first time. The dynamic and fast-paced game
of badminton is adored by our members, and we welcome new players who are ready to feel
the rush of competition.
Extra-curricular Activities

Basketball Club

Welcome to our Basketball Club, the center of hoops enthusiasm! Our club is the ideal location
for anyone wishing to shoot hoops or who is a
baller at heart. We are a group of passionate basketball players committed to sharing the love
of the game, developing player skills, building community, and having a blast. Whether your
goals are to become a three-point shooter, a
layup artist, or a better defensive player, our
team is dedicated to assisting you in realizing
your potential.
Extra-curricular Activities

Cooking Club

Welcome to the Culinary Canvas, a celebration
of all things delectable where fragrances, tastes, and creativity converge! With a passion
for cooking, our Cooking Club brings together a diverse group of food aficionados, from experienced home cooks to aspiring chefs. Join
our Cooking Club as we take you on a voyage of culinary exploration. We provide a range of
cooking lessons, workshops, and demonstrations that are appropriate for all skill levels, regardless
of experience level.
Extra-curricular Activities

Art Club

Welcome to the Palette Perspectives Art Club, where every brushstroke tells a different tale and creativity is unrestricted! Our club is a lively gathering place for artists and art aficionados to discover, be inspired by, and enjoy the vast realm
of visual expression. Join us and explore the
realm of artistic creation. All skill levels and
artistic styles are accepted, regardless of
whether you are an experienced artist or are just picking up a brush for the first time.
